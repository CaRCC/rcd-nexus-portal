Institutional Affiliation Request

If your institution is not part of the CILogon federation, you can still affiliate with it on the RCD Nexus, and use the tools here.

If you are the first user from your institution on RCD Nexus, and your institution does not support CILogon, the first step is to request a new institution.

If someone has already set up your institution enter your valid institutional email (e.g. ending with .edu) below and we will send you an email with a link to confirm your affiliation.
Note: If you do not have an institutional email, you will need to contact someone at the institution who is managing the associated RCD Profile, and ask them to invite you directly.
