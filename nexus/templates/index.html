Welcome to the RCD Nexus user portal!

Login

To access Nexus tools (including the RCD Capabitlities Model), you first need to login and create an institution profile.

Click the button below and find your institution in the list of CILogon providers, and then log in as you normally do.

If your institution is not listed, you can choose another identity provider like Google or Microsoft. Note: ORCID, GitHub, ACCESS, and ESNet do not work with the portal tools.

Institutional login

New to the Capabilities Model?

The Research Computing and Data Capabilities Model allows institutions to:

  • Assess their support for computationally- and data-intensive research,
  • Identify potential areas for improvement, and
  • Understand how the broader community views Research Computing and Data support.

Click HELP in the menu to learn more.
